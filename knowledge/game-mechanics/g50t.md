## Interaction Model
- **ACTION1 (UP)**: Moves player by (0, -1) * step_size via the game controller's `move()` method. Movement checks collision with maze and gates.
- **ACTION2 (DOWN)**: Moves player by (0, +1) * step_size.
- **ACTION3 (LEFT)**: Moves player by (-1, 0) * step_size.
- **ACTION4 (RIGHT)**: Moves player by (+1, 0) * step_size.
- **ACTION5 (UNDO/REPLAY)**: Triggers `pmlawcgvcp()` -- records the current move history, then begins undoing all moves in reverse. After full undo, the player is repositioned to start, a clone is created that replays the recorded path, and the next phase begins. The arrow indicator moves to highlight the next phase slot.

**Animation system**: The game controller (`qxlodtievc`) maintains `hjvvibklzv` (animation queue). While animations are active (`jqpwhiraaj` is True), `step()` processes animations rather than accepting player input. This is a blocking animation system.

**Multi-phase clone mechanic**: After ACTION5, the player's recorded moves are saved in `rloltuowth` as a dict mapping clones to move lists. On subsequent phases, clones replay their stored moves simultaneously with each player move. Phase count is determined by the number of `gpkhwmwioo` indicator sprites (varies per level: 2 in L1, 3 in L2-L3, etc.).

**Timer**: A scrolling sprite (`ppfvilwwnk`) moves left by 1 pixel every 2 steps (`ucorwtereb % 2 == 0`). When it scrolls fully off-screen (`-x > width`), the player loses.

## Level Progression
7 levels. Changes between levels:
- More phase indicator slots (more clones needed)
- More complex maze layouts with additional walls, gates, and enemies
- Ghost spawn points increase
- Maze structures become more interconnected with directional constraints

## No-Source Discovery Strategy
1. Press UP/DOWN/LEFT/RIGHT to observe grid-based movement (1-2 actions)
2. Notice phase indicators at top -- count them to know how many phases
3. Press ACTION5 and observe: player rewinds, clone appears replaying your path
4. Move to a different location in phase 2, press ACTION5 again
5. Observe both clones moving simultaneously with your phase 3 moves
6. Notice the scrolling timer bar at bottom -- understand time pressure
7. Locate the goal sprite (distinct color block) and navigate all clones toward it
8. Test what happens when a ghost touches you (lose condition)
9. Try completing all phases and reaching the goal with all clones alive

## Key Patterns
- Each phase records your moves. Clones replay exactly. Plan all paths simultaneously.
- Enemies (`vtwcsmdoqp`) move autonomously after each player step, following the nearest path direction.
- The timer is global -- every 2 steps it ticks. You must solve across ALL phases before time runs out.
- Collision is checked against walls (`hxztohfdlx`), gates (`kjrcloicja`), and maze boundaries.
- Gates and buttons likely interact -- activating a button may open/close a gate.
- The goal requires precise positioning: player center must align exactly with goal bounds.
- Undo (ACTION5) is the phase transition tool, not just a convenience feature.
