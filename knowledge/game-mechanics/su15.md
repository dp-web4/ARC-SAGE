# su15 — Suika Merge: Click to Vacuum Fruits Together, Merge Matching Sizes, Dodge Enemies

## Toolset
Available actions: [ACTION6=CLICK, ACTION7=UNDO].
Click-only game. Clicking a position triggers a radial vacuum that pulls all nearby fruits (and enemies) toward the click point. Undo reverts to the previous state. This is a 2048-style merge game with spatial/physics elements.

## Objects
- **Fruits** (tag `fruit`): Square sprites of increasing size, named "0" through "8". Sizes: 1x1 (color 10), 2x2 (color 6), 3x3 (color 15), 4x4 (color 11), 5x5 (color 12), 7x7 (color 8), 8x8 (color 9), 9x9 (color 7), 10x10 (color 14). Each fruit has a numeric "tier" (0-8). Two same-tier fruits merge into one fruit of the next tier. Tier 8 fruits that merge are removed entirely.
- **Enemies** (3 types, all 5x4):
  - `enemy` (tag `enemy`, color 7/orange): Type 1, speed 1. Chevron pattern.
  - `enemy2` (tag `enemy2`, color 14/yellow): Type 2, speed 1. Diamond pattern.
  - `enemy3` (tag `enemy3`, color 13/magenta): Type 3, speed **2** (moves twice as fast toward fruits).
- **Goal zones** (tag `goal`): Circle-shaped sprites (color 9/maroon), sizes 9x9 or 18x9. Named `avvxfurrqu`, `spnivuaouo`, `wovupizsya`. These are target areas where specific fruits/enemies must be delivered.
- **Key indicators** (tag `key`): Small colored sprites displayed at top of screen showing what needs to be in goal zones. Various shapes matching enemy/fruit patterns.
- **Hint arrow** (tag `hint`): Arrow sprite on level 1 pointing to a click location.
- **Vacuum ring overlay** (`gcpqtwbmkp`): 64x64 canvas sprite (layer default). Renders a ring at the current vacuum radius around the click point during animation.
- **Background elements**: `tixakbqato` (split 5/4 colored background), `vjbztqdvzs` (spiral of color 3 dots), various colored bars for UI.
- **Step counter UI**: Progress bar on row 63. Decremented by 1 per click (normal) or by 2+2*penalty per enemy collision flash. Per-level step budget from `data["steps"]`.

Constants: `bjetwxoaq=4` (vacuum frames/duration), `qjlubdgly=8` (vacuum radius), `stqbquzms=4` (move speed per frame), `gnexwlqinp=10` (min Y), `ncfmodluov=63` (max Y), `ftaeelrvuu=16` (flash animation frames).

## Interaction Model
- **CLICK (ACTION6)**: Click a position on the grid (must be y between 10 and 63). Triggers the "vacuum" animation:
  1. A circular ring appears at the click point with radius = `qjlubdgly` (8 cells).
  2. Over `bjetwxoaq` (4) frames, all fruits within the initial radius are pulled toward the click point. Movement speed = `stqbquzms` (4 cells/frame) toward center.
  3. Enemies also move -- type 1 enemies are pushed away (direction = away from click), type 2/3 chase nearest fruit each frame.
  4. The vacuum ring shrinks each frame.
  5. After all vacuum frames complete: **fruit merge check** via Union-Find:
     - All pulled fruits that are adjacent (touching) and have the SAME tier merge into one fruit of the next tier, placed at the centroid.
     - If two adjacent fruits have DIFFERENT tiers, a "flash" penalty occurs (costs extra steps).
     - Tier 8 fruits merging are removed from the game entirely.
  6. **Enemy merge check**: Enemies of the same type that touch merge into the next enemy type (enemy -> enemy2 -> enemy3). Different-type enemies touching cause a flash penalty.
  7. **Enemy-fruit collision**: Each frame, non-cooldown enemies chase the nearest fruit. If an enemy touches a fruit, the fruit gets "knocked" (bounces away, shakes for 4 frames, then either downgrades a tier or is removed if tier 0). The enemy gets a cooldown.
- **UNDO (ACTION7)**: Restores all fruit and enemy positions/tiers from the snapshot saved before the previous click. Clears the vacuum overlay.
- **Grayed out state**: When fruits can no longer reach the goal requirement (checked by `auvdgqbzgb()`), key indicators and goal zones turn color 2 (dark). Undo can recover from this.

## Win Condition
Method `kouxmshyjy()`: Reads the `goal` from level data. Goal format is either `[tier, count]` or `[[tier1, count1], [tier2, count2], ...]` where tier can be a fruit tier number or an enemy type string.
- Counts all fruits whose CENTER is inside a goal zone, grouped by tier.
- Counts all enemies whose CENTER is inside a goal zone, grouped by type.
- **Win = every goal entry is exactly satisfied.** E.g., `[3, 1]` means exactly 1 tier-3 fruit (4x4, color 11) must be centered on a goal zone.

Method `auvdgqbzgb()`: Checks if the total "value" (2^tier summed) of remaining fruits is sufficient to ever meet the goal. If not, the game grays out indicators (but doesn't auto-lose -- player must undo or lose on step depletion).

## Level Progression
**9 levels**, all on 64x64 grid. Changes:
- **Level 1**: 1 tier-2 fruit, 1 goal, 32 steps. Goal: [2, 1]. Tutorial with hint arrow.
- **Level 2**: 8 tier-0 fruits, 1 goal, 32 steps. Goal: [3, 1]. Must merge 0->1->2->3.
- **Level 3**: 6 tier-0 + 3 tier-1 fruits, 2 goals, 48 steps. Goal: [[3,1],[2,1]].
- **Level 4**: 8 tier-0 fruits, 1 goal, 1 enemy, 48 steps. Goal: [3, 1]. Enemy introduced.
- **Level 5**: 4 tier-0 + 4 tier-1 fruits, 1 goal, 2 enemies, 32 steps. Goal: [3, 1].
- **Level 6**: 1 tier-5 fruit, 2 goals, 1 enemy, 32 steps. Goal: [[3,1],[enemy_type1,1]]. Must deliver both a fruit AND an enemy to goals.
- **Level 7**: 4 tier-1 + 1 tier-5 fruits, 2 goals, 2 enemies, 32 steps. Goal: [3, 2].
- **Level 8**: 2 tier-3 + 1 tier-5 fruits, 4 goals, 3 enemies, 48 steps. Goal: [[4,2],[enemy_type2,1]].
- **Level 9**: 2 tier-1 + 1 tier-5 fruits, 3 goals, 4 enemies, 48 steps. Goal: [[4,1],[enemy_type3,1],[2,1]].

## No-Source Discovery Strategy
1. **First click**: Click somewhere near the colored squares (fruits). Observe them being pulled toward your click point.
2. **Observe**: Two same-size/color squares that collide merge into a larger square. The circle shapes on the board are goal zones. Key icons at the top show what size/color is needed.
3. **Hypothesis**: Merge small fruits into bigger ones by clicking to pull them together. Get the target-sized fruit onto a goal zone.
4. **Test**: Click between two same-colored fruits to pull them together and merge. Then check if the new fruit matches the key indicator.
5. **When ready**: Plan click positions to merge efficiently. Watch out for enemies (chevron shapes) that chase fruits and knock them down a tier. Use undo liberally.

## Key Patterns
- Fruit tiers: 0(1x1) -> 1(2x2) -> 2(3x3) -> 3(4x4) -> 4(5x5) -> 5(7x7) -> 6(8x8) -> 7(9x9) -> 8(10x10). Merging two same-tier = next tier. Tier 8+8 = removed.
- Vacuum radius = 8 cells, 4 animation frames, 4 cells/frame pull speed.
- Mismatched adjacent fruits/enemies after vacuum cause a FLASH penalty: costs 2 + 2*penalty_count steps (escalating).
- Enemies chase nearest fruit each vacuum frame. Collision knocks fruit down 1 tier (or removes tier-0).
- Enemy type 3 moves at speed 2 (double).
- Goal check requires CENTER of sprite to be inside goal zone circle.
- Y-bounds: fruits stay in rows 10-63. Row 0-9 is UI/key area.
- Undo saves snapshots before each click -- always available, no step cost.
- Color palette: 10=orange(tier0), 6=pink(tier1), 15=white(tier2), 11=cyan(tier3), 12=blue(tier4), 8=grey(tier5), 9=maroon(tier6), 7=orange(tier7), 14=yellow(tier8).
